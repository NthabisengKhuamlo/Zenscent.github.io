Meta Campaign Strategy

Strategic campaign development for Instagram + Facebook to drive engagement & reach Gen Z audiences.

Campaign Objective

Build an engaging Meta campaign designed to increase brand awareness, spark user interaction, and showcase the product experience in an authentic, Gen Z-friendly way. The strategy focuses on short-form content, engagement hooks, and modern visual aesthetics.

Key Strategic Pillars

  • Short-form Reels-first storytelling to match platform trends.
  • Behind-the-scenes product moments showcasing texture, scent & usage.
  • UGC-style content to boost trust and organic engagement.
  • Inbound aesthetic visuals to match Gen Z moodboard energy.
  • Weekly carousel series for education + storytelling.
  • Optimized posting schedule based on insights & peak engagement times.

Content Types Used

  • Instagram Reels + TikTok-style edits
  • Carousels with hooks and CTAs
  • Aesthetic product photography
  • Behind-the-scenes videos
  • Interactive Stories (polls, questions, quizzes)
