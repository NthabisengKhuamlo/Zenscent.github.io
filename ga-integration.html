Comprehensive tracking setup for Zenscents eCommerce platform

Project Type Analytics & Strategy
Duration 2 Months
Tools Used GA4, Looker Studio, GTM
Status Live & Optimizing

Project Overview

Set up comprehensive tracking for user behavior, conversion funnels, and traffic sources. Monitoring key metrics to optimize marketing efforts and measure campaign effectiveness across the Zenscents digital ecosystem.

This project involved implementing Google Analytics 4 (GA4) from the ground up, creating custom dashboards in Looker Studio, and establishing KPIs that directly tie to business objectives.

Google Analytics 4 Looker Studio Google Tag Manager Search Console Hotjar

The Challenge

As a new eCommerce brand launching in the competitive home fragrance market, Zenscents needed:

  • Complete visibility into user behavior from first visit to purchase
  • Understanding of which marketing channels drive the most valuable traffic
  • Identification of bottlenecks in the customer journey
  • Data-driven insights to optimize product pages and checkout flow
  • Ability to measure ROI on marketing campaigns and content efforts

Implementation Strategy

Phase 1: Foundation Setup

  • Created GA4 property with enhanced eCommerce tracking
  • Implemented Google Tag Manager for flexible event tracking
  • Set up custom events for key user interactions (Add to Cart, Begin Checkout, Purchase)
  • Configured conversion goals aligned with business objectives
  • Linked Google Search Console for SEO performance tracking

Phase 2: Event Tracking Configuration

Custom events implemented to track the complete customer journey:

Event Name Trigger Business Value
view_item Product page view Measure product interest
add_to_cart Item added to cart Track purchase intent
begin_checkout Checkout initiated Identify drop-off points
purchase Transaction completed Revenue attribution
newsletter_signup Email subscription Build marketing audience

Phase 3: Dashboard Creation

Built custom Looker Studio dashboards to visualize key metrics for different stakeholders:

  • Executive Dashboard: High-level revenue, conversion rate, and traffic overview
  • Marketing Dashboard: Campaign performance, channel attribution, and ROI
  • Product Dashboard: Top sellers, product page engagement, inventory insights
  • User Behavior Dashboard: Journey mapping, funnel analysis, and engagement metrics

Key Insights Discovered

Early Stage Growth Pattern

As a newly launched site, the data shows typical early-stage patterns with fluctuating traffic as organic presence builds. The 72.7% decrease compared to the previous period indicates this is the initial launch phase with varying testing and soft launch activities.

Strong Newsletter Conversion

With 19 newsletter signups from just 9 new users, the email capture strategy is performing exceptionally well. This 211% conversion rate (users signing up multiple times or from return visits) indicates strong interest and effective CTA placement.

Excellent Search Position

Achieving an average position of 6.2 on Google with 53 impressions and a 3.8% CTR shows strong SEO foundation. The site is ranking on page 1 for target keywords, which is impressive for a new domain.

High Engagement Per Session

With 18 page views from 11 sessions, users are viewing an average of 1.6 pages per session. Combined with 12 user engagement events, this indicates visitors are actively exploring the site rather than bouncing immediately.

Results & Impact

The comprehensive analytics implementation has enabled data-driven decision making across all marketing activities:

Quantifiable Achievements

  • Position 6.2 average ranking in Google Search results for target keywords
  • 3.8% CTR from search impressions, above industry average for new sites
  • 211% email conversion rate - 19 signups from 9 new users indicates strong value proposition
  • 1.6 pages per session showing engaged user behavior and effective navigation
  • 131 key events tracked providing comprehensive user journey visibility
  • Zero data loss - all user interactions properly captured since launch

Strategic Foundation Built

  • Established baseline metrics for post-launch comparison
  • Validated that newsletter signup strategy is highly effective
  • Confirmed SEO foundation is strong with page 1 rankings
  • Identified that users are engaged when they visit (multiple page views)
  • Created tracking infrastructure ready to scale with traffic growth
  • Enabled data collection for future A/B testing and optimization
